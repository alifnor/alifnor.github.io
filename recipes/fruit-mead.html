Fruit Mead

    Ingredients for 5 gallons

  • 17lbs of fruit of choice
  • 11lbs Clover Honey
  • Water 4.5 gallons
  • 2 1/2 tsp Pectic Enzyme
  • 5 tsb Tartaric Acid

    Directions

  • Smash up sound, ripe fruit, removing the pits.
  • Stir Honey into 4 or 5 gallons warm Water. Heat to boiling, and add Nutrient.
  • Add the tartaric acid and pectic enzyme, stirring well.
  • Rack away from the settlings, top up again, and let stand for two or three months.
